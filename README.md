# supply-chain-inventory-analytics

## Overview

The **Supply Chain & Inventory Intelligence Platform** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed to help organizations monitor inventory, evaluate supplier performance, optimize warehouse operations, and forecast future demand. The project combines **Python, PostgreSQL, Machine Learning, and Power BI** to transform raw supply chain data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and predictive analytics.

The platform addresses common operational challenges such as inventory shortages, excess stock, delayed deliveries, and inefficient procurement decisions by providing KPI-driven dashboards and data-driven recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle with:

* Overstocking and understocking of inventory
* Limited visibility into warehouse performance
* Poor supplier performance monitoring
* Increasing inventory holding costs
* Delayed shipments affecting customer satisfaction
* Difficulty forecasting future demand accurately

These issues lead to higher operational costs, stock-outs, delayed deliveries, and reduced profitability.

---

## Business Objective

Develop a comprehensive Supply Chain Intelligence Platform that enables businesses to:

* Monitor inventory health in real time
* Analyze supplier efficiency
* Evaluate warehouse performance
* Track product demand across regions
* Forecast future demand using Machine Learning
* Improve inventory planning through data-driven insights
